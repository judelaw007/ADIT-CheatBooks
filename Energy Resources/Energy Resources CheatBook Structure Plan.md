# ADIT Energy Resources (Module 3.04) CheatBook Structure Plan

## Executive Summary

This CheatBook Structure Plan outlines the development framework for the ADIT Energy Resources Option exam preparation guide. Based on comprehensive analysis of the 2025 syllabus and past examination papers (June 2025, December 2024, June 2024, December 2023), this plan identifies high-value topics, recurring question patterns, and optimal chapter allocation following the CheatBook methodology.

### 2.2 Question Archetype Classification

Based on past paper analysis, questions fall into these archetypes:

#### **Archetype 1: Fiscal Regime Comparison (25 marks - Part A)**
- Compare PSA vs Concession/Royalty-Tax
- Evaluate government take calculations
- Discuss advantages/disadvantages for IOC/NOC

#### **Archetype 2: Climate Change Policy Analysis (20-25 marks - Part A/B)**
- Carbon tax vs ETS comparison
- Border carbon adjustment mechanisms
- Methane abatement incentives
- Just transition considerations

#### **Archetype 3: Transaction Advisory (20-25 marks - Part A/B)**
- Tax due diligence checklists
- Acquisition structuring (share vs asset deal)
- Interest deductibility planning
- Post-acquisition integration

#### **Archetype 4: Transfer Pricing Dispute (15-20 marks - Part B/C)**
- OECD Guidelines application
- Comparables analysis
- MAP and arbitration procedures
- Documentation requirements

#### **Archetype 5: PE Risk Analysis (15-20 marks - Part B/C)**
- Construction/drilling PE thresholds
- Service PE exposure
- Agency PE considerations
- Treaty protection strategies

#### **Archetype 6: Financing Structure (15-20 marks - Part B/C)**
- Debt push-down mechanics
- Thin capitalisation rules
- Interest limitation rules (EBITDA)
- Hybrid instrument treatment

#### **Archetype 7: Contract Classification (15 marks - Part C)**
- Service contract types (Pure vs Risk)
- Joint venture vs contractor status
- Tax implications of contract choices
- Ring-fencing considerations
