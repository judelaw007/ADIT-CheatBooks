# ADIT United Kingdom Option - CheatBook

## Overview
This CheatBook is designed for the ADIT Module 2.09 (United Kingdom Option) exam, following the application-first, template-driven approach optimized for Bloom's Taxonomy levels 3-5 (Apply, Analyze, Evaluate).

## Exam Structure
- **Duration**: 3 hours
- **Total Marks**: 100
- **Pass Mark**: 50

### Question Breakdown
- **Part A**: 2 questions × 25 marks each (50 marks total)
  - Focus: Complex scenarios requiring comprehensive analysis
  - Topics: Residence, Permanent Establishment, Remittance Basis

- **Part B**: 1 question × 20 marks (20 marks total)
  - Focus: Anti-avoidance and international provisions
  - Topics: CFCs, Thin Capitalization, Transfer Pricing, Business Investment Relief

- **Part C**: 2 questions × 15 marks each (30 marks total)
  - Focus: Specific technical areas
  - Topics: Domicile, Trusts, ATED, Withholding Tax, and other miscellaneous topics

## CheatBook Structure

### 01. Battlefield Intelligence
Strategic exam intelligence including syllabus breakdown, past question patterns, and examiner preferences.

### 02. Major Question Types
Template-driven approach for each exam section (Parts A, B, C) with topic-specific frameworks and worked examples from past papers.

### 03. Cross-Cutting Skills
Essential skills that apply across all questions: time management, legislation analysis, calculation techniques, and answer structuring.

### 04. Execution Excellence
Mental frameworks and final preparation strategies for exam day performance.

### 05. Appendices
Quick reference materials: tax rates, allowances, key definitions, and acronyms.

## How to Use This CheatBook

1. **Phase 1 - Reconnaissance (Week 1-2)**: Study sections 01 and 05 for foundational understanding
2. **Phase 2 - Template Mastery (Week 3-6)**: Work through section 02, practicing templates with past papers
3. **Phase 3 - Integration (Week 7-8)**: Focus on section 03 to develop cross-cutting skills
4. **Phase 4 - Execution (Final Week)**: Review section 04 and do timed practice exams
