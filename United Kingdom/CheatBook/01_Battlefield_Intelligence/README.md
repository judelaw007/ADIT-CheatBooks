#### Week 1 of Study: Strategic Foundation
1. **Start with 1.2 Past Questions Analysis** (90 minutes)
   - Understand what topics actually appear in exams
   - Identify the "Big Three" critical topics

   - Map syllabus to priorities based on frequency data
